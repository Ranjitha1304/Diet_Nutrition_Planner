LENTIL CHILI

Lentils, tomatoes and ground meat are a winning combination in the lentil chili. This recipe packs 9 grams of fiber per saving. Flavorful and filling.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground meat (15% fat)
  • 1 1/2 cups onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (crushed)
  • 2 cups lentils (cooked, drained)
  • 2 cans tomatoes, low-sodium, diced or crushed (2g ounce or 2 cans 14 1/2 ounce)
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • cumin, ground (1/2 teaspoon, optional)

Directions

  1. Wash hands with soap and water
  2. In a large saucepan, brown meat over medium-high heat, breaking it into bite sized pieces. Drain fat.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  4. Add lentils, tomatoes, chili powder and cumin. Cook for about 1hour until flavours are blended.
  5. Serve hot, topped with your favourite chili toppings.
